About

Now in its 4th year, our Annual Women in Federal IT and Cyber Event brings together the nation’s top Federal IT executives to celebrate the success of women thriving in today’s federal technology industry through videos, social media, agency-specific events, and more. The event will also promote federal IT job openings to help attract new IT talent.

Our Goals

Our goals are to:

  • Celebrate women working in federal information technology by highlighting their stories and their accomplishments.
  • Coach women just starting out in Federal IT by sharing career advice on STEM pathways in federal service.
  • Collaborate and connect by hosting events to celebrate women in their IT workforce.
