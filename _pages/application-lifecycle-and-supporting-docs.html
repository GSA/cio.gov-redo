Policy Overview

The Application Lifecycle Framework - and associated template documents - provide high-level guidance for moving applications from on-premise locations to cloud environments. The steps and templates included in this framework provide a notional list of tasks for your agency’s application migration but should not be considered a mandatory checklist of requirements for every agency or for every application.
