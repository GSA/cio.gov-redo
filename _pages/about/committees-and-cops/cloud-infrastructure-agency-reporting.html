Agency Reporting

In accordance with the Federal Information Technology Acquisition Reform Act (FITARA), each CFO Act agency must:

  • Submit to OMB a quarterly DCOI Inventory Report as part of the Integrated Data Collection (IDC). Review the guidance on MAX to learn how to complete and submit this report.
  • Publish in machine-readable JSON format an annual DCOI Strategic Plan that describes the agencyâ€™s consolidation and optimization strategy.
  • Report publicly on the IT Dashboard a quarterly DCOI Inventory report and Annual DCOI Strategic Plan.
