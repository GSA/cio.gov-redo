4.11 Performance Improvement Council (PIC)

The PIC, a government-wide body that supports cross-agency collaboration and best practice sharing, was established under Executive Order 13450 (The White House. Executive Order 13450 - Improving Government Program Performance. 11/13/2007) in 2007 and codified in law under the GPRA Modernization Act of 2010 (PIC. Performance Improvement Council). The PIC is chaired by the Deputy Director for Management at OMB within EOP. The membership of the PIC includes PIOs and associated staff from federal agencies. (PIC. About the Council.) PIC.gov is where the Council shares priorities, news, and information about key performance management topics. (Ibid).
